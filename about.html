The Most Comprehensive Audit & Compliance Platform for Indian Financial Institutions

It combines audit automation and compliance workflow management into one comprehensive platform, helping financial institutions streamline audits, ensure RBI compliance, and reduce operational risk.

What Sets Komplieye Apart

Unlike generic tools, Komplieye is purpose-built as an audit automation platform and RBI compliance software for Indian financial institutions. Our enterprise-grade approach delivers end-to-end coverage with tightly integrated modules.

AI-Driven Intelligence

Our AI analyzes RBI circulars and automatically generates actionable compliance controls. New regulations trigger policy updates and task assignments seamlessly.

Unified Integration

All modules are tightly integrated - audit findings automatically trigger compliance tasks, creating a seamless workflow from identification to resolution.

Banking-Specific Design

Pre-configured with RBI guidelines and banking best practices. Ready-to-use templates, workflows, and compliance frameworks from day one.

Enterprise Security
