Audit Management System (AMS)

Komplieye's Audit Management System empowers your internal audit team to plan, execute, review, and report on audits with unprecedented efficiency and control. From multi-branch audit scheduling to one-click reporting, the AMS module digitizes the entire audit lifecycle.

Risk-Based Audit Planning & Scheduling
Create audit plans and schedules for all branches and departments. Assign main auditors and assistants, set priorities based on risk, with built-in conflict checks and automated reminders.
End-to-End Audit Workflow Automation
Complete audit lifecycle support with template-based checklists, real-time progress tracking, and automated audit trails for transparency.
Collaboration & Review Process
Built-in Review Module with dedicated dashboards for approvals, contextual comments, and digital workflow ensuring audit quality and consistency.
Audit Reporting & Analytics
One-click comprehensive reports with trend analysis, audit archives, and real-time visibility for data-driven risk mitigation decisions.

Seamless Integration Between AMS & CMS

Komplieye's AMS doesn't stop at identifying problems – it helps you solve them. Audit findings and observations can be instantly converted into remediation tasks assigned to the relevant business owners. The moment an audit uncovers an issue, the system creates a task in the Compliance module, complete with details and due dates.

Audit Finds Issue
Auditors identify compliance gaps or policy violations during audits
Auto Task Creation
System automatically creates compliance tasks with details and deadlines
Track to Closure
Tasks are tracked through resolution with full accountability

Role-Based Access Control

Both AMS and CMS modules support comprehensive role-based access control, ensuring different audit and compliance team members see and do only what their role permits.

Main Auditors
Can plan and submit audits with full access to audit modules
Assistant Auditors
Work on assigned sections with limited access to their specific tasks
Reviewers
Approve or send back audits with review permissions
Compliance Officers
Manage compliance tasks and regulatory requirements
