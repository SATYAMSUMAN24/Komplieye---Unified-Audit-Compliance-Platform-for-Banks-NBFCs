Komplieye is a comprehensive Audit Management System (AMS) and Compliance Management System (CMS) provided by Kalolytic Solutions Pvt. Ltd. for banks and NBFCs operating in India.

Platform Services Include:
  • Risk-based audit planning and execution
  • Automated compliance monitoring and tracking
  • RBI return preparation and submission assistance
  • AI-powered regulatory analysis and alerts
  • Centralized document management and reporting
