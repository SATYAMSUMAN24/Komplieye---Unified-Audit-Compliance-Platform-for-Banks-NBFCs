Real-World Applications

How Banks & NBFCs Use Komplieye

Versatile Platform, Multiple Solutions

Komplieye is a versatile platform that adds value across numerous audit and compliance scenarios in your organization. Here are concrete examples of Komplieye in action:

Facing an RBI Audit or Inspection

When regulators like the Reserve Bank of India come for an inspection, Komplieye has you fully prepared. All your compliance activities, audit reports, and evidence are organized and readily accessible in the platform.

During RBI Inspection:
  • Instant Access: Need to show your audit trail or status of a past finding? It's available in seconds.
  • Real-time Logging: Any observations can be immediately logged as tasks and assigned to appropriate owners on the spot.
  • Auto-extraction: Letter of Inspection (LOI) and observation reports can be uploaded directly - Komplieye auto-extracts findings into tasks.
Outcome: Demonstrate a proactive stance to regulators and ensure no regulatory finding is left unaddressed post-inspection.

Streamlining Internal Branch Audits

Imagine your internal audit team conducting routine audits across dozens of branches. Komplieye standardizes and streamlines this entire process.

How It Works:
1
Centralized Programs: Audit programs and checklists can be centrally defined and distributed via the platform
2
Real-time Recording: Auditors record findings in Komplieye, which branch managers can view in real time
3
Automated Assignment: Tasks are automatically assigned to branch managers for corrections
4
Remote Verification: Branch managers upload proof of correction, auditors verify remotely
Result: Auditing dozens of branches becomes as easy as auditing one. Save time and travel costs while improving oversight.

Regulatory Return Filing: No More Missed Deadlines

Banks and NBFCs often juggle numerous regulatory filings – quarterly returns, annual statements, ad-hoc reports – each with strict deadlines.

Example Scenario: RBI Quarterly Return Due in 10 Days
1

Compliance Calendar already has this event scheduled and visible

2

Team members collaborate using predefined templates

3

System assigns sub-tasks to department heads (Treasury, Finance)

4

Automated reminders sent as due date approaches

5

One-click generation and API upload to RBI portal

Result: Zero missed deadlines and stress-free reporting process. No more last-minute scrambling.

Tracking New RBI Circulars & Implementation

Regulatory changes can come fast and frequently. Komplieye ensures no regulatory change remains just paper knowledge.

Example: RBI Issues New KYC Circular
  • AI Analysis: System analyzes the circular and identifies impact on Customer Onboarding policy
  • Task Generation: Creates specific tasks - update policy, revise checklists, staff communication
  • Smart Assignment: Tasks assigned to appropriate owners with AI-derived instructions
  • Progress Tracking: Dashboard shows implementation status across all circulars
  • Closure: Compliance Officer marks circular as "Implemented" once all actions complete
Outcome: Swift and systematic operationalization of regulatory updates. Confident compliance with new requirements.

Closing the Loop on Audit Findings

Every audit finding can spawn a compliance task with full tracking to resolution. Nothing gets lost in emails or forgotten in file cabinets.

Example: Control Weakness in Loan Approval Process
1

Auto-Task Creation: "Address finding: Missing second-level approval in loan process"

2

Assignment: Task assigned to Branch Manager and Credit Head

3

Action & Evidence: Responsible parties update task and attach evidence

4

Review & Closure: Audit team reviews evidence and closes task

5

Knowledge Base: Builds repository for similar future issues

Impact: Closed-loop remediation system ensuring every audit finding is tracked to resolution. Creates culture of continuous improvement.
