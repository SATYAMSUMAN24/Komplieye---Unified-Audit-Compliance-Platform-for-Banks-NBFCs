Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Komplieye, operated by Kalolytic Solutions Pvt. Ltd., collects information necessary to provide audit and compliance management services to financial institutions.

1.1 Business Information
  • Institution name, license numbers, and regulatory identifiers
  • Contact information of authorized personnel
  • Audit schedules, findings, and compliance data
  • RBI return data and regulatory correspondence
1.2 Technical Information
  • IP addresses and system logs for security monitoring
  • User authentication data and access patterns
  • Platform usage analytics for service improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Service Delivery: Providing audit management and compliance tracking services
  • Regulatory Compliance: Assisting with RBI return preparation and submission
  • Security: Monitoring for unauthorized access and ensuring data integrity
  • Support: Providing technical assistance and customer service

3. Data Security

Enterprise-Grade Protection
  • ISO 27001:2013 certified data centers
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication and role-based access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • CERT-In compliance for Indian financial institutions

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or regulatory authorities (RBI, SEBI, etc.)
  • With your explicit written consent
  • To authorized service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • In case of legal proceedings or fraud investigation

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as per RBI guidelines and industry standards:

  • Audit Records: 7 years from completion date
  • Compliance Data: As per respective regulatory requirements
  • User Logs: 12 months for security monitoring
  • System Backups: 90 days with encrypted storage

6. Your Rights

Access Rights
Request copies of your personal data we hold
Correction Rights
Request correction of inaccurate information
Deletion Rights
Request deletion (subject to regulatory requirements)
Portability Rights
Request data export in machine-readable format
